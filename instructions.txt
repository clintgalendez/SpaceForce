********************************************Instructions***************************************************
Before playing the main game, you are tasked to place 20 spaceships in the board. Strategize where you will
put it, as it will help make your spaceship less likely to be bomped by the enemy.
***********************************************************************************************************
The player will take turns with the CPU. The player is tasked to guess and use visual clues to target the
Zomocronians spaceships in the board. Each spaceship destroyed will be added on the score, on the other
hand, for every time the enemy hits your spaceships will result on a subtracted points. The first one to
destroy all ships win.
***********************************************************************************************************
